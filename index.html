About us

Hopla Ice Skating Club would like to provide a space for those who wants to move their muscles in a fun way and have a nice time with family/friends. Hopla is a great place to experience the beauty of this winter sport and experience it everyday of the year.

On the webpage you can find information about our club, prices, special events, a gallery where you can gain insight into Hopla life, and where you can contact us.Have a great surfing!
